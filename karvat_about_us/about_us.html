The Founding Story

In the summer of 2012, our founder, Ananya Sharma, witnessed a silent crisis in the heart of Vidarbha. Skilled artisans and visionary farmers were losing their livelihood to a lack of market access and modern tools.

Named after the traditional weaving comb, "Karvat" represents the intricate interlacing of old-world wisdom with modern opportunity. What started as a small community workshop has grown into a nationwide movement for rural empowerment.
